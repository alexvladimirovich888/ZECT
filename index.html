Essential Privacy Tips

  • Use unique passwords: Avoid reusing the same password across different platforms.
  • Two-factor authentication: Always add an extra layer of security.
  • Limit permissions: Apps don’t need access to everything on your phone.
  • Think before sharing: Your personal data is forever once it’s online.
  • Encrypt your storage: Lock your drives and backups.
